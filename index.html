WELCOME TO SEA VIEW GOLF!

Sea View Golf is Set on 670 acres of Galloway, NJ, coast and woodlands, Seaview is home to two world class golf courses and a 296-room hotel. The Bay Course opened in 1914 and was designed by Hugh Wilson and Donald Ross. The 6,300-yard layout is less a test of length, and more a test of imagination, accuracy and short game. The Bay Course presents a Scottish-links feel, dramatic seaside views, deep pot bunkers and small undulating greens. It is the current host of the ShopRite LPGA Classic, as well as nine holes in the 1942 PGA Championship.

ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

SEA VIEW RANGE

Sea View Golf Range in New Jersey offers a scenic and relaxing experience for golf enthusiasts. Situated with a beautiful view of the water, it’s perfect for both beginners and experienced golfers who want to practice their swing or enjoy a day out in nature.

EAT & DRINK

Eat and Drink Specials at Sea View Golf Range offer a variety of delicious options to complement your day on the green. The on-site café provides golfers with a selection of snacks, sandwiches, and light meals, perfect for a quick bite before or after your session.

WEDDINGS

Weddings at Sea View Golf Range offer a picturesque and intimate setting for your special day. Nestled in the beautiful New Jersey landscape, the venue provides a stunning backdrop of rolling greens and scenic views, perfect for wedding ceremonies and receptions alike.
