WELCOME TO SEA VIEW GOLF!

Sea View Golf is set on 670 acres of Galloway, NJ, coast and woodlands. Seaview is home to two world-class golf courses and a 296-room hotel. The Bay Course opened in 1914 and was designed by Hugh Wilson and Donald Ross. The 6,300-yard layout is more a test of imagination and accuracy than length. It presents a Scottish-links feel with dramatic seaside views, deep pot bunkers, and small undulating greens.
